About Me

Hi! I'm Mark. I'm a web developer with experience working in various client-facing roles. Prior to software development, I studied Marketing and Business Analytics at Indiana University and worked in management consulting for a couple years. These experiences have given me broad exposure to many different parts of a business, from working with customers to managers to development teams.

It was during this time that I got to see programming in action for the first time. From there, I started to do more exploration on my own. I enjoyed the feeling of getting code to work for the first time and when new concepts were beginning to click.

After several months of self-study, I made the decision to go all in on learning to code to make this career a full-time possibility.

I'm excited to understand more deeply the tools I use on a daily basis, as online has become an integral part of daily life for many.

In addition to coding, some of my other interests include travel, music, and language learning.
