About Me

Hi, I'm Mark. I'm a software developer with experience in various client-facing roles. Prior to software development, I studied Marketing and Business Analytics at Indiana University and worked in management consulting for a couple years. These experiences provided me with broad exposure to the many different parts of a business.

It was during this time that I first got to see programming in action. From there, I started to do more exploration on my own. I enjoyed the feeling of getting code to work for the first time and of new concepts that were starting to click.

After several months of self-study, I decided to go all in on learning to code in order to make this career a full-time possibility. I'm excited to understand more deeply the tools I use daily and apply my knowledge towards building experiences for the everyday user.

In addition to coding, a few of my other interests include travel, music, language learning, and watching football.
