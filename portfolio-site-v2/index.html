About Me (**need to implement smooth scroll**)

Hi! I'm Mark. I'm a web developer with experience in various business roles and working with clients. Prior to software development, I studied Marketing and Business Analytics at Indiana University and worked in management consulting for a couple years.

It was during this time that I got to see programming it in action for the first time. From there, I started to do more exploration on my own, and I enjoyed the feeling of getting code to work for the first time and whenever a new concept I was learning started to click.

After several months of self-learning, I made the decision to go all in on learning to code to make this career a full-time possibility.
